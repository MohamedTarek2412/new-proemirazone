Benefits of Free Zone Setup

100% Foreign Ownership

Complete ownership and control of your business without the need for local sponsors or partners, providing full autonomy over your operations and decisions.

Tax Exemptions & Profit Repatriation

Enjoy corporate tax exemptions, custom duty benefits, and the freedom to repatriate 100% of your profits and capital to your home country without restrictions.

Fast Registration Procedures

Streamlined and efficient company registration processes with quick turnaround times, allowing you to start your business operations in the UAE rapidly.
