Mainland Company Formation in UAE

Establish your business with full market access across all Emirates

Establishing a Mainland company allows full access to the UAE market and the flexibility to operate across all Emirates. Proemirazone guides clients through licensing, approvals, and post-formation services, ensuring a seamless setup.

Our consultants analyze your business needs and recommend the most suitable structure and Emirate to maximize operational efficiency.

Benefits of Mainland Setup

Full UAE Market Access

Complete access to the entire UAE market without restrictions, allowing you to conduct business freely across all seven Emirates and directly with government entities.

Business Flexibility Across Emirates

Operate seamlessly across all Emirates with the ability to establish multiple branches and conduct business throughout the UAE without geographical limitations.

Local Partnerships & Licensing Options

Access to local sponsorship opportunities and various licensing options including commercial, professional, industrial, and tourism licenses tailored to your business needs.

Our Process & Support

Trade Name Registration

Comprehensive trade name registration and initial approval process to secure your business identity and ensure compliance with UAE naming conventions.

  • Trade name reservation and approval
  • Initial approval from DED
  • Name compliance verification
  • Multiple name options assessment

Legal Document Drafting (MOA/LSA)

Professional drafting of Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Local Service Agent (LSA) agreements to establish clear business frameworks and partnerships.

  • MOA preparation and notarization
  • LSA agreement drafting
  • Legal document attestation
  • Partnership agreement setup

Office Lease & Ejari Registration

Assistance in securing appropriate office space and completing Ejari registration to fulfill physical office requirements for mainland companies.

  • Office space selection and leasing
  • Ejari contract registration
  • Location approval verification
  • Facility management coordination

License Issuance & Employee Visas

Complete support for trade license issuance and comprehensive employee visa processing to build your operational team in the UAE.

  • Trade license application and issuance
  • Chamber of Commerce registration
  • Employee visa processing
  • Labor card applications
